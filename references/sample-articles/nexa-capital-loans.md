# Need An Avionics Loan? Ask Nexa Capital

**Source:** Aviation Consumer
**Author:** Larry Anglisano
**Date:** August 12, 2016
**Category:** Shop Business / Financing

Nexa Capital's "Jumpstart loan program" aims to provide financing for avionics projects, responding to a demand from buyers looking for such loans. Nexa Capital works with established lenders to offer avionics loans at commercial rates, and it educates these lenders on the specific demographic of avionics buyers.

The program is presented as a convenient way to get funding for avionics upgrades, as it's an avionics-specific loan program that doesn't require borrowers to justify the investment in their aircraft to a general loan officer. Nexa claims to streamline the process, sourcing borrower funding quickly (less than one week) and handling payments directly with the installing shop.

Although a more attractive interest rate might be in the 4 percent range, borrowers have the option of using the aircraft as collateral for a better rate within the program. While a partnership with the Department of Transportation (DOT) for nearly interest-free loans didn't materialize, Nexa independently moved forward with its program.
