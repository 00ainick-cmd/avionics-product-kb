# Avionics Product Taxonomy

Master list of product categories, manufacturers, and specific equipment for the knowledge base.
Products marked with ✅ have Larry Anglisano source material available.
Products marked with 🔲 need original research.

---

## 1. GPS/Navigation

### Garmin
- **GTN 750Xi** — Touchscreen GPS/Nav/Comm, flagship navigator ✅
- **GTN 650Xi** — Compact touchscreen GPS/Nav/Comm ✅
- **GNS 530W** — Legacy GPS/Nav/Comm (still widely installed) ✅
- **GNS 430W** — Compact legacy GPS/Nav/Comm ✅
- **GPS 175** — Budget GPS navigator ✅
- **GNX 375** — GPS with ADS-B transponder built in 🔲

### Avidyne
- **IFD550** — Touchscreen navigator (GNS 530 slide-in replacement) ✅
- **IFD540** — Previous gen touchscreen navigator ✅
- **IFD440** — Compact touchscreen navigator (GNS 430 replacement) ✅

### BendixKing (now Honeywell)
- **AeroNav 900** — Touchscreen GPS/FMS/NAV/COM (GNS slide-in replacement) ✅
- **KSN 770** — GPS/Nav/Comm 🔲
