# Garmin GMA345: Slide-and-Fly Audio Panel

**Source:** Aviation Consumer
**Author:** Larry Anglisano
**Date:** November 2018
**URL:** https://aviationconsumer.com/safety/garmin-gma345slide-and-fly-audio-panel/
**Category:** Audio Panels
**Products covered:** Garmin GMA345, GMA342, GMA340, PS Engineering PMA8000G, PMA450B

---

The GMA345 is Garmin's upgraded audio panel priced at $1,895, designed as a direct successor to the popular GMA340. It maintains backward compatibility with existing GMA340 wiring, significantly reducing installation complexity and costs.

## Key Features

The unit supports up to six positions with voice-activated stereo intercom. It retains the same rugged push-button controls as the GMA340, with green annunciators and photocell brightness control. Dual rotary knobs manage pilot and copilot/passenger volume.

## Bluetooth Connectivity

Pairs with one device at a time (A2DP profile for audio; HFP for telephone). Remembers up to 10 paired devices. Two separate wired music inputs are available. USB Type-A charging port outputs 2.1 amps.

## Advanced Audio Processing

3D Audio creates positional separation between comm radio sources in stereo headsets. Avionics Squelch (ASQ) filters background hiss from VHF communications. A 60-second digital clearance recorder enables read-back verification. The music equalizer offers presets (classical, pop, rock) and bass boost options.

## Technical Specifications

Dimensions: 6.3 x 1.33 x 8.09 inches. Weight: 1.78 pounds. Standard configuration: Two VHF comms, two nav radios, telephone input. Three-comm version available upon request.

## Headset Compatibility Note

The panel requires stereo audio jacks for proper stereo functionality. Using monaural headsets in stereo jacks shorts the right channel to ground, resulting in left-channel audio in both ears. This doesn't damage equipment but affects audio separation.

## Pricing

GMA345: $1,895 (with Bluetooth). GMA342: $1,695 (without Bluetooth). The $200 premium for Bluetooth functionality is deemed worthwhile.

## Assessment

A sizable jump in technology from the GMA340, with praised build quality and audio improvements. Installation diagnostics via extended PLAY button hold assist troubleshooting. Recommended for hands-on comparison demonstrations with the PS Engineering PMA8000G ($2,100) and PMA450B.
