# What's the Best Display Upgrade?

**Source:** Aviation Consumer
**Author:** Larry Anglisano
**Date:** May 2019
**URL:** https://aviationconsumer.com/industry-news/editorial/whats-the-best-display-upgrade/
**Category:** EFIS/PFD / Flight Displays
**Products covered:** Garmin G3X Touch, G500/600 TXi, G5, Aspen Evolution MAX, Dynon SkyView HDX Certified

---

This article examines three major glass display retrofit systems: Garmin's G3X Touch, Aspen's MAX display upgrade, and Dynon's Certified SkyView HDX. It represents an unusually competitive market period in avionics upgrades.

## Decision-Making Approach

Selecting the right system requires first determining if you want an all-electric avionics setup. The buying decision mostly comes down to how you adapt to the user interface. Hands-on demonstration is essential before purchasing — flying the system in realistic conditions beats observing trade show demos.

## Feature Priorities

Integrated electronic engine data is highlighted as a must-have, including complete replacement of mechanical gauges, temperature sensors, and fuel quantity displays. This eliminates unreliable analog instruments across the panel.

All major systems offer synthetic vision, traffic display, weather graphics, and mapping — except the G5. Touchscreen capability is standard except on Aspen's Evolution system.

## Five Total Options

When including Garmin's TXi and G5 upgrades, five retrofit options exist. All three primary systems (G3X Touch, Aspen MAX, Dynon HDX) are STC'd to replace vacuum systems entirely, representing a significant modernization opportunity.

## Conclusion

There is no slam-dunk best electronic display upgrade, but the refreshingly competitive marketplace offers worthy alternatives. Each system has strengths that favor different aircraft, budgets, and pilot preferences.
