# Nighthawk Flight Systems Guardian Integrated Flight Deck
**Source:** AEA Avionics News (September 2025)

The Nighthawk Guardian is a modern integrated flight deck designed for Part 23 Class I/II aircraft, as well as experimental and restricted-category aircraft. It features a unique modular architecture centered around a "NEST" hub.

- **Guardian Displays:** Available in 12-inch (11.6 inches of screen area) and 7-inch touch configurations.
- **NEST (National Electronics System Tray):** A centralized hub that racks Line Replaceable Units (LRUs).
- **N101000 LRU:** Handles analog-to-digital conversion and system interconnects.
- **MCU (Mission Control Unit):** Supports up to two displays; an additional MCU in the NEST can support up to four displays.

- The NEST system is 50% lighter than traditional integrated systems, weighing under 5 pounds with three LRUs installed.
- The NEST can be mounted anywhere within 25 feet of the displays.
- Field support is simplified with "blade links" (plastic jumpers) that allow for fast and simple plug-in swaps of LRUs.

- **Certification:** FAA certification for the basic 12-inch system and initial LRUs is expected in Q4 2025 (Part 23 Class I/II).
- **Pricing:** Basic System Under $40,000. Fully Integrated Multiscreen System Approximately $110,000 (includes four displays and two complete NESTs).

The Guardian system is designed to be compatible with a wide variety of digital and analog autopilots, excluding Garmin models.
