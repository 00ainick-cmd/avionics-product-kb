# Audio Panel Upgrades: PS Engineering is Tops

**Source:** Aviation Consumer
**Author:** Larry Anglisano
**Date:** July 2009
**URL:** https://aviationconsumer.com/avionics/audio-panel-upgrades-ps-engineering-is-tops/
**Category:** Audio Panels / Intercoms
**Products covered:** PS Engineering PMA8000B, PMA7000B, PMA6000, PMA4000, PMA9000EX, Garmin GMA340, GMA347, GMA240, Honeywell KMA28, BendixKing KA134, VAL AP100

---

Modern audio panels function as comprehensive control hubs managing communications radios, traffic and terrain alerts, intercoms, and cabin entertainment systems. Success depends far more on quality installation than brand selection.

## Installation

Proper installation typically costs around $4,000 for parts and labor, including new wiring and audio jack housings. Shortcuts in wiring are unacceptable in the high-stakes game of cabin audio.

## Garmin Products

The GMA340 ($1,895) features an integral six-seat intercom with independent pilot/copilot controls. Supports three comm radios and two warning inputs. It automatically mutes music during radio transmissions, though a modification can address this.

The GMA347 adds automatic squelch, full-duplex cell interface, and digital voice recorder. Installation requires extra programming effort.

The GMA240 ($895) is a non-TSO four-place panel designed for LSA and experimental aircraft, offering basic radio switching and entertainment controls.

## PS Engineering Lineup

The PMA8000B ($1,895) is the flagship unit with six-place IntelliVox stereo intercom, dual coms/navs, ADF, marker beacon, and dual-channel entertainment. The PMA8000B-MP3 ($2,095) adds 1GB built-in MP3 player. The PMA8000-SR ($2,590) integrates SIRIUS Satellite Radio receiver.

The PMA7000B ($1,595) features IntelliVox intercom, three-com capability, split-com function, and marker beacon. Can directly replace older BendixKing KMA24 units. The PMA7000CD ($2,399) adds remote CD/MP3 player capability.

The PMA6000-Series ($995) is the budget entry-level switching panel. The PMA4000 ($849) is a compact two-inch format suitable for tight spaces and tandem aircraft.

## Other Options

Honeywell KMA28 is essentially a rebranded PS Engineering PMA7000 at higher cost. BendixKing KA134 ($2,573) provides basic switching only, no intercom. VAL Avionics AP100 ($495) is a minimal one-inch height switching panel.

## Recommendations

The PMA8000-series emerges as the top choice, with the PMA8000B-MP3 recommended for convenient built-in music management. For budget-conscious pilots, the GMA340 remains proven and reliable. Address electrical system issues before undertaking audio upgrades, as poor power quality significantly degrades audio performance.
